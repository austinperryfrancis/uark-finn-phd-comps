# Li et al. (2021)

Paper: [[Li, Mai, Shen, and Yan 2020]]
Week: 13 - Innovation and machine learning

## Front
Li et al. (2021)
Measuring Corporate Culture Using Machine Learning

## Back

### Research Question
Can machine learning measure corporate culture from firm language, and does measured culture matter for firm behavior and performance?

### Construct
Corporate culture = firm-level emphasis on innovation, integrity, quality, respect, and teamwork, measured from earnings-call language using word embeddings.

### Data
Earnings-call transcripts linked to firm financials, executive compensation, earnings management, M&A activity, and firm outcomes.

### Design/ID
Uses machine learning to score firm culture from earnings-call text and validates the measures against firm behavior, performance, compensation design, and changes around mergers.

### Findings
Machine-learning culture measures capture meaningful differences across firms. Culture is related to operating efficiency, risk-taking, earnings management, compensation design, firm value, and deal activity, with stronger culture-performance links in bad times. The paper makes corporate culture measurable at scale.
