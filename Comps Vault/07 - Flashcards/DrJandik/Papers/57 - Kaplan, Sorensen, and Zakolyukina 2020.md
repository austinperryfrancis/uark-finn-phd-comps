# Kaplan et al. (2022)

Paper: [[Kaplan, Sorensen, and Zakolyukina 2020]]
Week: 15 - Governance

## Front
Kaplan et al. (2022)
What is CEO overconfidence? Evidence from executive assessments

## Back

### Research Question
What does the Longholder measure of CEO overconfidence actually capture?

### Construct
CEO overconfidence = Longholder option-exercise behavior; executive-assessment traits measure overconfidence, ability, charisma, and other personality characteristics.

### Data
CEO personality and ability assessments linked to option-exercise behavior, investment, cash flow, and firm characteristics.

### Design/ID
Compares Longholder CEOs to detailed executive-assessment measures to test whether Longholder captures overconfidence, ability, or other CEO traits; then examines investment-cash flow sensitivity.

### Findings
Longholder CEOs score higher on traits associated with overconfidence but also lower on ability-related traits. Longholder CEOs have higher investment-cash flow sensitivity, but overconfidence remains distinct from ability after controlling for assessments. The paper clarifies that Longholder captures both overconfidence and some lower-ability CEO characteristics.
