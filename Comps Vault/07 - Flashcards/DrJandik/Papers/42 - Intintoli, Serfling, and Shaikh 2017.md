# Intintoli et al. (2017)

Paper: [[Intintoli, Serfling, and Shaikh 2017]]
Week: 11 - Suppliers and customers

## Front
Intintoli et al. (2017)
CEO Turnovers and Disruptions in Customer–Supplier Relationships

## Back

### Research Question
Do CEO turnovers at major customers disrupt supplier relationships and harm supplier performance?

### Construct
Customer disruption = CEO turnover at a major customer; supplier exposure = dependence on that customer relationship.

### Data
U.S. customer-supplier relationships linked to customer CEO turnovers, supplier sales, operating performance, and stock returns.

### Design/ID
Tests whether suppliers experience worse outcomes after CEO turnover at a major customer; separates routine retirements from more disruptive turnovers and examines whether effects are stronger when suppliers depend more on the affected customer.

### Findings
CEO turnover at major customers disrupts customer-supplier relationships and harms suppliers through lower sales, weaker operating performance, and negative stock returns. Effects are stronger when the customer relationship is more important and when turnover is more disruptive, showing that managerial changes can transmit shocks through supply chains.
