# Sautner et al. (2023)

Paper: [[Sautner et al. 2023]]
Week: 12 - Climate finance

## Front
Sautner et al. (2023)
Firm-Level Climate Change Exposure

## Back

### Research Question
How can researchers measure firm-level exposure to climate change, and do those exposures matter for real outcomes and market prices?

### Construct
Climate change exposure = share of earnings-call discussion devoted to climate-related topics; separates overall climate exposure into opportunity, physical, and regulatory exposure.

### Data
Global earnings-call transcripts linked to green jobs, green patents, option prices, stock returns, and firm characteristics.

### Design/ID
Uses a machine-learning keyword discovery algorithm to identify climate-change bigrams in earnings calls; validates the measures with human audits, industry patterns, emissions, green innovation, and asset-pricing tests.

### Findings
Climate exposure varies mostly at the firm level, even within industries. The measures predict green job creation, green patenting, and information priced in options and equity markets, showing that earnings-call language captures firm-specific climate exposure beyond emissions.
