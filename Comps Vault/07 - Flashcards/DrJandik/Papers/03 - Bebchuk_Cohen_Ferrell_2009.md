# Bebchuk et al. (2009)

Paper: [[Bebchuk_Cohen_Ferrell_2009]]
Week: 1 - Corporate governance: issues and indices

## Front
Bebchuk et al. (2009)
What Matters in Corporate Governance?

## Back

### Research Question
Which governance provisions in the broad GIM/IRRC index actually matter for firm value and returns?

### Construct
Entrenchment index/E-index = six provisions: staggered boards, limits to shareholder bylaw amendments, poison pills, golden parachutes, supermajority merger requirements, supermajority charter amendment requirements.

### Data
U.S. firms with IRRC governance provisions; firm value and stock return data.

### Design/ID
Regressions of Tobin’s Q and abnormal returns on E-index while controlling for the other IRRC provisions and firm controls; also uses 1990 entrenchment and firm FE tests.

### Findings
Higher E-index is associated with lower Tobin’s Q and lower abnormal returns; the six E-index provisions drive the GIM-index relation, while the other eighteen provisions do not explain much.
