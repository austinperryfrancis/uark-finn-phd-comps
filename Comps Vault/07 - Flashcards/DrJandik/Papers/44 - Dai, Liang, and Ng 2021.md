# Dai et al. (2021)

Paper: [[Dai, Liang, and Ng 2021]]
Week: 11 - Suppliers and customers

## Front
Dai et al. (2021)
Socially responsible corporate customers

## Back

### Research Question
Do socially responsible customers influence the CSR behavior of their suppliers?

### Construct
Customer CSR influence = pressure from high-CSR customers on suppliers’ environmental and social practices; supplier CSR = suppliers’ own CSR performance after forming customer relationships.

### Data
U.S. customer-supplier relationships linked to CSR ratings, supplier/customer characteristics, relationship importance, and firm outcomes.

### Design/ID
Tests whether suppliers improve CSR after forming relationships with high-CSR customers; uses relationship initiation, customer importance, and supplier bargaining power to distinguish customer influence from simple matching.

### Findings
Suppliers improve CSR after forming relationships with socially responsible customers, especially when the customer is important and the supplier has less bargaining power. CSR influence propagates through supply chains and is associated with better supplier efficiency and valuation, showing that customer pressure can transmit responsible corporate behavior.
