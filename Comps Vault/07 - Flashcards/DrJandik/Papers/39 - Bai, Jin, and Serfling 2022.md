# Bai et al. (2022)

Paper: [[Bai, Jin, and Serfling 2022]]
Week: 10 - M&A: sources of synergies

## Front
Bai et al. (2022)
Management Practices and Mergers and Acquisitions

## Back

### Research Question
Do mergers create value by transferring better management practices from acquirers to targets?

### Construct
Structured management practices = more specific, formal, frequent, or explicit practices related to monitoring, production targets, and incentives.

### Data
U.S. Census establishment-level management-practice data linked to acquisitions, firm characteristics, and establishment performance.

### Design/ID
Tests whether more structured acquirers buy less structured establishments and whether acquired establishments adopt more structured practices after acquisition; uses acquirer ability/incentives and target-acquirer complementarity to test the mechanism.

### Findings
More structured acquirers tend to buy less structured establishments, and targets adopt more structured practices after acquisition. Changes are larger when acquirers have greater ability or incentive to impose better practices, and adoption is associated with improved establishment performance, supporting management-practice transfer as a source of M&A synergies.
