# Babina et al. (2024)

Paper: [[Babina, Fedyk, He, and Hodson 2024]]
Week: 13 - Innovation and machine learning

## Front
Babina et al. (2024)
Artificial intelligence, firm growth, and product innovation

## Back

### Research Question
Does firm investment in artificial intelligence affect firm growth, and through what channel?

### Construct
AI investment = firm-level hiring and worker exposure to AI-related skills; product innovation = new or improved products associated with AI adoption.

### Data
Firm-level employee resumes and job postings linked to firm growth, product innovation, market valuation, and industry concentration.

### Design/ID
Builds a firm-level AI-investment measure from labor-market data and uses exposure to AI universities as an instrument for firms’ AI adoption.

### Findings
Firms investing more in AI experience higher sales growth, employment growth, and market valuations. The growth comes mainly through product innovation rather than simple cost cutting, and AI-related gains are concentrated among larger firms, increasing industry concentration.
