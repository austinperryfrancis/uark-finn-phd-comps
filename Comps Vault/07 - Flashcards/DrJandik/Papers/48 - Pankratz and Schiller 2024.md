# Pankratz & Schiller (2024)

Paper: [[Pankratz and Schiller 2024]]
Week: 12 - Climate finance

## Front
Pankratz & Schiller (2024)
Climate Change and Adaptation in Global Supply-Chain Networks

## Back

### Research Question
How does physical climate exposure affect firms and supply-chain relationships, and do customers adapt by changing suppliers?

### Construct
Supplier climate exposure = realized heat or flood exposure at supplier locations relative to expected historical exposure; supply-chain adaptation = termination and replacement of supplier relationships.

### Data
Global supply-chain links from FactSet Revere linked to firm financials, supplier/customer locations, temperature data, flood exposure, and climate projections.

### Design/ID
Tests whether supplier heat and flood exposure affect supplier/customer performance; then examines whether customers terminate suppliers after unexpectedly high exposure and replace them with less exposed suppliers.

### Findings
Heat at supplier locations reduces supplier and customer operating performance. Customers are more likely to terminate suppliers after unexpectedly high climate exposure and choose replacement suppliers with lower expected and realized exposure, consistent with supply-chain adaptation.
